King's College London Engineering Society

We are the oldest collegiate society of our kind in the world.


Founded in 1847, our society welcomes all students interested in the advancement and future of engineering.

Who We Are

We're an interest group of engineers at King's College London. Our committee is mainly comprised of engineering students, and general members from all disciplines at King's.

What We Do

Our aim is to make engineering accessible to everyone. With close communication to engineering staff at King's, we shape the future of our own education, and gain access to cutting-edge equipment for our learning.
